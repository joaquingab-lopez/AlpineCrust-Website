Alpine Crust Shipping Policy

Thank you for choosing Alpine Crust! To make sure your pastries arrive fresh and on time, please take a moment to review our delivery guidelines.

I. Shipping

Rates are automatically calculated at checkout based on address and volume. Please provide complete and accurate address to avoid delays. We deliver through trusted third-party couriers. Please note that these riders do not represent Alpine Crust, and we are not responsible for delays caused by traffic or courier availability.

II. Shipping Dates

  • - Pre-orders are scheduled based on the calendar date you select at checkout.
  • - Blocked-off dates mean we are fully booked and cannot take more orders.
  • - All pastries are baked and dispatched fresh on your chosen date.

III. Dispatch & Coverage

  • - Orders are dispatched between 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM on your selected date.
  • - Actual arrival depends on traffic, courier availability, and delivery distance.
  • - We currently deliver within Baguio City and select nearby areas (please check during checkout if your address is serviceable).

IV. Important Notes

Riders are given handling instructions, but Alpine Crust cannot be held liable for delays or damages during transit. In case of incidents, you may report them to us so we can assist in coordinating with the courier service.
