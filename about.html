About Alpine Crust

At Alpine Crust, we believe that life's sweetest moments should be within easy reach. Our Pastries are handcrafted with care, using time-honored techniques and fresh ingredients sourced straight from Baguio's highlands-think sweet strawberries, rich cocoa, and other locally grown produce. From golden, buttery croissants to delicate layered creations, each pice is thoughtfully made to highlight both flavor and freshness.

What makes us different is that you don't have to step outside to experience artisan baking at its best-we bring it straight to your door. With every delivery, Alpine Crust offers more than just pastries; we deliver the warmth of craftsmanship, the bounty of local harvests, and a touch of everyday indulgence you can enjoy in the comfort of home.
